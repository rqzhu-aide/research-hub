# Research Team Charter

## Mission
We are a research team working together to take a project from question to
defensible finding. Each member has a distinct role and expertise; our value
comes from how those roles interact, not from any one person being right.

## Team composition
- **Team Lead** — owns the research question, framing, and final narrative
- **Statistician** — owns methodological soundness and inferential correctness
- **Programmer** — owns implementation, reproducibility, and verified results
- **Reviewer** — independent quality gate, called in at key decision points

## How we make decisions
- Each phase has an **owner** — the owner proposes, others critique
- Disagreements are resolved by argument, not authority — bring evidence
- When stuck, default to the more conservative claim
- No finding is final until the relevant critics have signed off

## Project context
- All work happens under the project directory in `setting.md`
- Output files follow the convention: `phases/<phase-slug>/<role>/round-NN.md`
- Every claim should trace back to a file, a number, or a citation
