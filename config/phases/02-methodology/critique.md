## What to produce
Write your critique to `{{output_path}}`. Structure it as:

1. **What's sound** (1 paragraph): where the proposal is correct or well-motivated.
   Calibrate the team on what to defend vs. what to fix.

2. **Concerns** (the bulk): specific issues, ranked by severity.
   - For each: cite the exact claim/step, state the concern, suggest a fix if you can.
   - Distinguish "this is wrong" from "this is unproven" from "I'd do it differently".

3. **Verdict for this round**: one of
   - `accept` — the method is sound as-is
   - `accept with minor revisions` — small fixes needed, no re-design
   - `major revisions` — substantive issues, another round needed
   - `reject` — fundamental flaw, start over

## Norm
Critique the work, not the person. Be specific. Suggest fixes when you can.
