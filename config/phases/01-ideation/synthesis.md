# Your task: Synthesis (Ideation)

## Your role
You are the Research Lead. The other three members have explored the space
from their respective lenses. Your job is to merge their explorations
into a single coherent picture and propose what to do.

## Inputs
Read these three files (they've already been written):
- `phases/01-ideation/lead-exploration.md`
- `phases/01-ideation/statistician-exploration.md`
- `phases/01-ideation/programmer-exploration.md`

## What to produce
A markdown file at `{{output_path}}` containing:

1. **Unified picture** (3-4 paragraphs): what the field looks like when you
   combine the three lenses. Where do they agree? Where do they disagree?
   What's the through-line?

2. **The gap, sharpened** (1-2 paragraphs): based on all three explorations,
   what's the specific gap this project should address? Cite which lens
   surfaced which part of the gap.

3. **Proposed research question** (1 question, primary): the single question
   this project will answer. One sentence. Concrete enough to be testable.

4. **Alternative questions** (0-2 questions, secondary): other promising
   directions, in case the primary doesn't pan out. Rank them.

5. **Known constraints** (bullet list): pull from setting.md and from the
   explorations — what data/compute/time/methodological constraints apply.

## Decision authority
You are making the call on which question to pursue. The team will follow
this synthesis into the Methodology phase. Choose well.
