# Phase: Ideation

## Goal
Explore the literature and problem space from three lenses, then synthesize
into a coherent picture that identifies a gap worth pursuing.

## Pattern: Parallel
All three members explore **independently and simultaneously** — you do not
see each other's work during this phase. After all three explorations are
complete, the Team Lead synthesizes them into a unified view.

## Output convention
- Each member writes to: `phases/01-ideation/<role>-exploration.md`
- Synthesis writes to: `phases/01-ideation/synthesis.md`

## What "exploration" means here
This is *not* a full literature review. It's a focused scan to answer:
1. What's been done in this space?
2. What's the current frontier?
3. Where's the gap that this project could fill?

Aim for signal, not exhaustive coverage. ~5-10 high-quality references per
member is plenty; more is not better.
