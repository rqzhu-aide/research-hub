# Your task: Lead exploration (Ideation)

## Your lens
You are exploring from the **domain significance** angle:
- What questions in this space actually matter to the field?
- What's the current consensus, and what's contested?
- Where do practitioners / researchers most feel the gap?

## What to produce
A markdown file at `{{output_path}}` containing:

1. **Lay of the land** (2-3 paragraphs): what's the field, what's the current
   state, what are the big open questions

2. **Key works** (5-8 items): the papers / projects that define the field.
   For each: one sentence on what it established, one on why it matters.

3. **The gap** (1-2 paragraphs): what's missing that this project could
   address. Be specific — "more efficient" isn't a gap, "no method exists
   for X under constraint Y" is.

4. **Candidate questions** (1-3): concrete research questions this project
   could answer, phrased as actual questions.

## Style
Write as if for a smart colleague in an adjacent field. No jargon dumps.
Lead with the gap; the lit review exists to motivate it.
